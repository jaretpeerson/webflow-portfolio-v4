Case Study

Therapy West

For Therapy West, I led marketing design for seven Utah locations, creating a nature-inspired website, print campaigns, and digital assets. Together, these multi-channel efforts helped bring in over 1,100 new and 1,200 returning patients.

Project Overview

Therapy West is a multi-location clinic in Utah dedicated to helping patients recover, strengthen, and thrive through expert physical therapy. When they approached this project, the main challenge was a lack of cohesive marketing across their seven locations, with outdated materials that failed to capture the brand’s energy and patient-centered mission.

The goal was to create a unified, nature-inspired brand experience that spanned web, print, and digital channels, driving both new and returning patient growth. By refreshing the visual identity and aligning messaging, the project aimed to boost engagement, strengthen brand recognition, and increase clinic visits.

The final designs combined clean layouts with organic, welcoming visuals to reflect Therapy West’s focus on healing and movement. From the redesigned website to the coordinated print campaigns and digital assets, every element worked to make the brand approachable, professional, and easy for patients to connect with.
